## 1. The Irresistible Offer Creator

**Prompt:**
"Act as an offer engineering specialist who has built Grand Slam Offers for live business events generating 10x more registrations from the same audience. Engineer the complete irresistible offer for BUSINESS UNLOCKED — a full-day summit for Filipino MSE owners, startup founders, and solopreneurs doing P500K–P50M/year. Stack every value element (4-Pillar Framework, 5 toolkit outputs, networking with 2,000 entrepreneurs, expert speakers), eliminate every obstacle between the attendee and their transformation, remove every risk from their decision, and make the total offer so valuable that P1,999 feels embarrassingly small. Event: BUSINESS UNLOCKED. Niche: Filipino entrepreneurs navigating disruption."

**Insights for the sales page:**
- Frame the event as a complete business transformation system, not just a seminar
- Stack the 5 toolkit outputs as standalone assets each worth more than the ticket price
- Emphasize: "You're not paying for a seat — you're investing in a complete crisis-proof business blueprint"
- Anchor the total value of everything included (workbook + maps + plans + networking + speakers) at P25,000+ of value
- Remove friction: "Doors open at 8AM, everything provided, just show up ready to build"

## 3. The Value Stack Designer

**Prompt:**
"Act as a value stacking specialist who has designed event offer stacks making competing seminars feel criminally overpriced for delivering less. Design the complete value stack for BUSINESS UNLOCKED at P1,999 Early Bird. List every deliverable (The Cashflow Blueprint workbook, Presence Map, Hybrid Sales Plan, Adaptability Canvas, 7-Day Action Card), bonus (networking with 2,000 entrepreneurs, expert speakers, accountability partner), and convenience element (venue, materials, all-day access) in the exact sequence making each addition feel more valuable than the last. Show exactly why P1,999 feels like a fraction of what the stack is genuinely worth. Core offer: Full-day event with 5 outputs. Price point: P1,999 Early Bird."

**Insights for the sales page:**
- Present each toolkit item with an anchored value:
  - The Cashflow Blueprint (core workbook): P5,000 value
  - Presence Map workshop: P3,000 value
  - Hybrid Sales Plan session: P3,500 value
  - Adaptability Canvas workshop: P3,500 value
  - 7-Day Action Card + accountability partner: P2,000 value
  - Networking with 2,000 entrepreneurs: P5,000 value
  - Expert speaker sessions: P5,000 value
- Total anchored value: P27,000+
- "You're getting P27,000+ of business transformation tools for P1,999"
- Present in pricing section as "Total Value" vs "Your Investment"

## 7. The Offer Strategy Architect

**Prompt:**
"Act as an offer strategy architect who has designed complete event offer ecosystems making every competing seminar feel like an inferior version. Design the complete offer blueprint for BUSINESS UNLOCKED. Include the core offer (Regular) as the obvious category leader, the entry offer (Early Bird) converting price-sensitive entrepreneurs into believers, the premium offer (VIP) for total transformation, and the single element competitors cannot honestly copy — making the stack permanently defensible. Business: BUSINESS UNLOCKED. Niche: Filipino entrepreneurs navigating disruption."

**Insights for the sales page:**
- Tier positioning:
  - **Early Bird** = "The smart move" — same experience, lower price, rewards decisive action
  - **Regular** = "Full access" — the complete experience
  - **VIP** = "The unfair advantage" — everything Regular gets + the tools and access that separate leaders from attendees
- Defensible element: The 4-Pillar Framework + 5 built-in-the-room outputs is unique — no competitor offers a hands-on system you build and take home
- Rename pricing badges for more punch:
  - Early Bird → "EARLY BIRD — Limited"
  - Regular → "FULL ACCESS"
  - VIP → "THE UNFAIR ADVANTAGE"
- Add urgency: "Early Bird pricing ends [date] or when first 500 seats fill — whichever comes first"
